Privacy Policy for Notiq

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Notiq does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your notes and data are stored locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

The app uses local storage on your device to save your notes. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

3. Permissions

Notiq requires internet permission only for app updates and potential future features like cloud sync (which would be optional).

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete all your notes at any time by uninstalling the app.
